Color Palette Creator

You could be designing a kitchen, picking the perfect colors for your next business idea, or simply enjoy creative AI assisted prompting and colors then this tool is for you.

You could feed them into Anthropic's Claude, OpenAI's ChatGPT, or X's Grok. Even output files ready to go for your favorite no code environment like base44 or Lovable. Give your upstream AI the context it needs to be consistent.
